What the project signals

The public repository is sparse, but the framing is clear: this was an attempt to make ebook publishing a WordPress behavior rather than a separate downstream process. That is a useful clue to the larger publishing instinct running through the archive.

Instead of treating a post or page as something that ends at the browser, the plugin imagines WordPress as a system that can help content travel into additional formats.

Why it matters here

This page belongs in the Writing track because the writing practice here has always had an infrastructural side. Publishing is not only authorship. It is also formatting, packaging, distribution, and the software choices that determine how a text moves.

Even as a small and early project, WP eBookit is part of that longer line of thinking: writing systems are also software systems.

Core Idea

Move ebook production closer to the CMS instead of leaving it as an external publishing afterthought.
